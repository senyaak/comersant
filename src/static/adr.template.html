What are ADRs?

Architecture Decision Records (ADRs) capture important architectural decisions made during the development of the Comersant platform. Each record documents the context, decision rationale, and consequences of significant technical choices.

These records serve as a historical reference and help current and future developers understand why certain design decisions were made, what alternatives were considered, and what trade-offs were accepted.
